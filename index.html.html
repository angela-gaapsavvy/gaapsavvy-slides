TOPIC 2
Partner / Reseller — Evidence of End-Customer Purchase
Eunice (partial session)
Under partner reseller deals, is evidence of the end-customer's purchase required? If so, what form is sufficient — and is this a RevRec requirement or a controls requirement? For co-sell (gross basis): is an email from the end customer with start/end dates and price sufficient above a materiality threshold?
Co-Sell (Gross Basis)
Evidence FormEmail confirmation from end customer (not from partner)
Sufficient if includesStart/end dates · product · price — above materiality threshold
Key PointEmail must be FROM end customer directly
Reseller (Net Basis)
Evidence FormPO or order form from partner typically sufficient
Core QuestionEnd-customer evidence may only be a controls requirement, not RevRec
ThresholdMateriality threshold applies throughout
Open to the Group
Question 1Is this RevRec or controls? How do you distinguish?
Question 2What are peers accepting in practice?
Question 3What has your Big 4 auditor accepted / pushed back on?
